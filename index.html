Our Donation Plan

When floods strike, your donation helps to provide shelter, food and comfort to families

Food for a family

We provide food to the families who are affected by flood. During flood there are lack of food and clean water. We deliver them foods and water during the disaster.

Shelter for a family

We ensure shelter for the flood affected people. All the houses go under water in a flooded area. They need safe shelter. We contribute on their shelter process with your money.

Education for a child

We give books and papers, pens and dresses to the childrens after a flood. Their books and paper get ruined during the flood. They need help to buy books, paper, pen and dresses.
